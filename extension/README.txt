MonoFocus Website Blocker

Chromium requires you to confirm extension installation.

1. Extract this ZIP to a permanent folder.
2. Open chrome://extensions or edge://extensions in your Chromium browser.
3. Enable Developer mode, choose Load unpacked, and select the extracted folder.

Keep the MonoFocus desktop app running. The extension receives only the enabled
website blocklist and whether a focus session is active through a private local
connection on your computer.
